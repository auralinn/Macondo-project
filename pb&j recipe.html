Instructions

Get your stuff ready. Get a plate and place the bread on it. You are going to need a butter knife which you would use to spread the jam and peanut butter. Next, spread your jam on one slice of the bread. Do the same to the other slice with peanut butter. The last step is just putting it all together. Hold each slice in a hand and make them kiss. Make sure the corners are matching up. That's it, you've just made a PB&J sandwich.
