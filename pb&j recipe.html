PB&J

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Jam
  • Peanut butter

Instructions

Get your station ready. Get a plate and place the bread on it. Next you are going to need a butter knife which you would use to spread the jam and peanut butter. Next, spread your jam on one slice of the bread. Do the same to the other slice with peanut butter. The last step is putting it all together. Hold each slice in a hand and make sure the slices meet together in the middle. Make sure the corners are matching up. That's it, you've just made a PB&J sandwich.
