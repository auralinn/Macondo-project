Instructions

First of all, gather all your ingredients. After that, whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and a pinch of salt together in a bowl until its is well combined and smooth.
Butter a pan, or griddle but don't do it too much. Then heat the pan over medium-high heat. cover each slice of the bread into the mixture for around 5-10 seconds per side. We recommend brioche bread, but take any bread of your choice. Make sure the pieces are thick, but not too thick.
Transfer it to the hot pan or griddle and cook until golden brown and slightly crisp. About 3-4 minutes per side(You can use a timer for that if you want). Serve it hot and enjoy some butter or syrup with it or both!
