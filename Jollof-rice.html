Instructions

We start off by blending the spices. We do that by placing the garlic and ginger into a blender, then adding about a quarter cup of water. Blend it into a paste.

Let's season the chicken. Start by placing the chicken into a pot. Add in about one quarter of the diced onion. Then add in two tablespoons of blended garlic and ginger, half a teaspoon of the white pepper, one and a half teaspoons of salt, and 2 teaspoons of chicken bouillon powder. Turn or toss everything to combine.

Cooking the chicken. Add 3 cups of water to the chicken. After that bring to a boil on high heat then let it simmer on medium heat for about 18 minutes.

Let's cook the tomatoes. Do this by adding fresh tomatoes to a blender and blend it into a paste. Don't add water to the blender. Pour the blended tomatoes into a medium saucepan. Let it cook on medium heat for 20 minutes until the tomatoes shrink. Don't stir while the tomatoes cook down.

Now let's prep the chicken for frying. Remove the chicken from its stock using a frying spoon and place it in a colander to drain. Reserve the stock. Reserve about 2 cups of the stock.

Fried chickennn. Place oil in a frying pan up to 2 inches. Let the oil heat up, then add the chicken to the oil and fry until golden brown. When one side turns golden, flip to the other side.

Remove the fried chicken and place it on a paper towel to absorb the excess oil. Let the oil cool down, then take out one cup of oil to cook the rice.

Sauce for the rice. Place one cup of the oil that was used in frying the chicken into a non-stick or heavy-bottom pot. Let the oil heat up, then add the remaining onions.

Sauté for two minutes while stirring from time to time until the onions are fragrant.
Add the cooked-down tomatoes to the pot and cook while stirring for about 5 minutes.

Add the tomato sauce and cook while stirring from time to time until the sauce shrinks and starts sticking to the bottom of the pot. Then add in the one teaspoon of dried thyme and stir so it releases its flavor.

Add in the remaining blended garlic and ginger. Stir it well and let it cook for about 2 minutes. Wash the rice 3-4 times to remove excess starch. After that, add the washed rice to the pot with tomato sauce. Stir it well to combine.

Now, let's add in the reserved 2 cups of chicken stock, the remaining one teaspoon of salt, two teaspoons of chicken bouillon powder, half a teaspoon of white pepper, and 2 bay leaves. Stir everything to combine.

Add in 2 cups of water. Cover the pot tightly with a large sheet of aluminum foil(this helps to trap the steam in the pot). Lace the lead on the pot and reduce the heat to low.

Cook the rice on low. Let the rice cook on low heat for 20 minutes. Open the pot and gently stir the rice, being careful not to break the grains.

Cover the rice again with aluminum foil and place the lid of the pot on the aluminum foil(Watch the video to see what I mean.)

Allow the rice to cook on low heat for another 20 minutes.

It's time to eat our veggies, so do the veggie dance. Open the pot and add the diced carrots and green beans. Cover and let the veggies cook with the rice for 5 minutes.

Fluff and serve. Remove bay leaves, fluff rice with a fork, and serve with the fried chicken. You could serve with an additional salad if you so desire(which I recommend).
